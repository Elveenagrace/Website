TIMINGS

BEFIT is committed to providing convenience and flexibility to accommodate our members' busy lifestyles. Our gym operates on a schedule designed to cater to various preferences, ensuring that you can find a suitable time to work out. Here are our gym timings:

Monday to Friday:

Morning Session: 6:00 AM to 10:00 AM

Midday Session: 12:00 PM to 3:00 PM

Evening Session: 5:00 PM to 9:00 PM


Saturday and Sunday:

Morning Session: 7:00 AM to 11:00 AM


We understand the importance of a balanced work-life schedule, so we offer early morning, midday, and evening sessions throughout the weekdays. On weekends, we provide a slightly later start time to allow our members to enjoy a rejuvenating workout at their own pace.

Please note that these timings are subject to change based on the evolving needs of our members and the local community. We are dedicated to providing a supportive and accessible fitness experience, and our team is always ready to assist you during our operational hours.
